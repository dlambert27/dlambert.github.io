Alien World

This is a theoretical alien world. For my alien world, I wanted to go for a hidden base in the mountains sort of vibe. The picture focuses on a rope bridge leading into the mountains and the base. The backdrop is supposed to be violent, chaotic winds with the ghostly remains of life looming in the background.

Character Design

Freeg

This is a character design that I made called Freeg. Freeg is a simple creature, merely just trying to survive day to day and trying to get enough food for the day. For this character design, I originally wanted to go for a more "Sock Puppet" style of creature (Beady eyes, simple features, round mouth). In the end, I decided on turning this character into a plant-like creature in a pot. I thought it would look funnier than just a head poking out of the ground. For this design, I wanted to practice with general creature design. My style tends to lean more towards horrific, stylized art, so I wanted to transfer that into a more simplistic, cuter design.

Askr

This is a character design based on Yggdrasil, the World Tree from Norse Mythology. The animal parts are inspired by the various animals that live on Yggdrasil. For this character design, I wanted to challenge myself by making a humanoid creature.

Creature Design

Impeode

Impeode is a terrestrial planet that was once a very similar planet to Earth. It once hosted a large variety of life, including a highly-intelligent, ground-dwelling species that dominated the planet. However, due to numerous meteor showers, it has transformed into what it is today.
Meteoruptera is a omnivorous species of insect that is in the universe of Meteorum. They are characterized by their large wings, colorful wing tassels, and their probiscis-like beak. They can grow up to a foot in length, however Escorts can grow tens of times larger, around 20 - 30 feet in length. The males are a deep red, while the females are a muted brown. Their legs are either black or yellow, with claws ranging from a bright yellow to a light green. Their wings' colors are synonymous with what nest their affiliated with. Their wings also have long wing tassels that look like the tail of a comet.
